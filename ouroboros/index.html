The process

Three steps.
Better outputs.

01
Type naturally

Write your prompt the way you always do. On ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot — anywhere. Ouroboros watches quietly in the background.

02
See the improvement

Click the Ouroboros trigger. In seconds, see an improved version with a word-level diff showing exactly what changed and why.

03
You decide

Use the improved prompt, keep your original, or edit before sending. Nothing goes anywhere without your explicit approval. Always.
